Video

When creating the video, I first downloaded the .zip file provided by the tutor and unzipped it. I then opened video editor. After clicking on "New Video Project" a new window appeared to rename my video file. Then on "Project Library Section", I clicked on "+" sign which allowed me to choose the photos that spelled my MIT ID (MIT332996). After that, I selected the photos and added them to "story board", which makes the photos into video in sequence of the order that the photos are kept. I added some transitions,filters, 3D effects and music to the video and the video was ready.
Transitions are specially important when edition/creating a video because they provide a smooth and clean transfer to next frame without making the video choppy and enhancing user experience.
